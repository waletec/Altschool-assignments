Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A timeless Italian pasta dish with rich, savory meat sauce served over tender spaghetti. Perfect for a comforting family dinner.

Recipe Details

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, sauté until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Drain pasta and serve topped with the sauce. Garnish with basil.

Tips

Tip: For deeper flavor, simmer the sauce for 1.5–2 hours and use a splash of red wine when cooking the beef.
